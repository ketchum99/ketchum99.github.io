Planets, Vehicles and Weapons

From Tatooine to Coruuscant from blasters to lightsaber and from an x-wing to the slave 1, this section has many planets, vehicles and weapons in the Star Wars universe.

Introduction

This section will blow your mind with 'out of this world' informaton, not only with space vehicles but with land speeders, weapons and many of the different planets in this universe. Most of the information here comes from The Stupendous Wave, Star Wars explained and the Wookiepedia.

The Galaxy

The Star Wars galaxy is made up of over 100 billion stars and is 120 lightyears in diameter. It's home to nearly 100 quadrillion lifeforms, these lifeforms decided to break up the galaxy into separate parts. These parts, from the centre are called the deep core, the core worlds, the colonies, the inner rim, the expansion region, the mid rim, the outer rim. wild space and the unknown regions.

Vehicles

There are many different types of vehicles in Star Wars, however instead of making a bunch of different vehicles, there is usually one vehicle and different variants of it. A good example of this is the TIE (Twin Ion Engine) Fighter, it has many variants, the TIE Bomber, Heavy TIE Bomber, TIE Interceptor, TIE exp, TIE Crawler, TIE Defender, TIE Raptor, TIE Lander, TIE Phantom and so many more variants of the same ship. This goes the same for most of the other craft in Star Wars.

Weapons

The Star Wars weapons consists of a large arsenal of close range, long range and so many blasters. To start off, the iconic Light Saber is an extremely powerful blade of plasma (as said by the games and The Game Theorists) that can cut through almost any material. The force could be considered a weapon, but not everyone can use it instantly, some people can't even use it. But the blasters are the real weapons that anyone can use. Some examples are the A280C, CA-87, DH-17, DL-44, DLT-19, E-11, EE-3, RT-97C, SE-14C, T-21, T-21B, DL-18, DLT-19X, Relby-V10, DT-12 and so, so many more.
