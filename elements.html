People, Species and Movies

Movies

There are currently 7 Star Wars movies in the franchise: The Phantom Menace (Episode I), Attack of the Clones (Episode II, Revenge of the Sith (Episode III), A New Hope (Episode IV), The Empire Strikes Back (Episode V), Return of the Jedi (Episode VI) and The Force Awakens (Episode VII). Episode IV-VI were made first, with director George Lucas, Episodes I-III were filmed after the first 3 but took place before the original trilogy and these movies were directed also by George Lucas. Although the prequels (most people would agree) are the worst Star Wars movies ever made, or even the worst Star Wars content ever, maybe it's because there are many mistakes, they used lots of CGI or mabye because they are just boring movies. After Disney bought Star Wars on October 30th 2012 for $4.05 billion, Disney said that they would make 3 more Star Wars movies, The Force Awakens, Rogue One and one more unknown one. Episode VII did not disapoint, it looked great had everything great about Star Wars and had great CGI where it was needed. Many people have high hopes for Episode VIII, and we all hope it will do the same, and blow us away with another great Star Wars experience.

Wookiee

The Wookiee are a humanoid species native to the planet Kashyyyk. They are tall, strong and are covered by soft, thick fur. Wookiees are known as ferocious foes and loyal friends. The average Wookiee grows over 2m high and has a much larger life span than a human.

Human

Nobody quite knows where humans originated from, or how they spread across millions of worlds. Their home planet is thought to be Coruscant. Humans are a big part of the galaxy, mostly in politics as Humanity is not a hated race by the Galactic Empire.

Hutt

The Hutts are big a big slug like race with large eyes, wide mouths and stubby hands. They originated from the planet Varl. They can see not only in visible light, but in ultraviolet light too. The Hutt species do not have bones, rather an internal mantle that holds up their head and allows them to move their arms etc; a shark is a good example of this.

Bith

The Bith are a race of aliens with big skulls and long fingers. They are the same species that played at the cantina in Episode IV. The race is known for abstract thinking and lacks instinctual emotion like fear or passion. They have huge black eyes but no eyelids, as they have evolved past the need to sleep.

Sullustan

The Sullustans are a race of mouse like humanoids with large almond shaped black eyes. They originated from the planet Sullust. They are experts in technology, manufacturing and technological development. They have appeared in some Star Wars movies, usually as rebel fighters, and have also appeared in Star Wars Battlefront (EA) as playable rebel fighters.
